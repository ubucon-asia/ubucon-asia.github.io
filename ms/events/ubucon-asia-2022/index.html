UbuCon Asia 2022

  • Date: 2022-11-26 ~ 2022-11-27
  • Venue: Nuritkum Square (Nuri Dream Square)
  • Location: Seoul, South Korea

First In-person UbuCon Asia will be hold in Seoul, South Korea

Introducing the first In-person UbuCon in Asia, UbuCon Asia 2022

With venue sponsorship from NIPA(National IT Industry Promotion Agency),
The event will be hold at Nuritkum Square, Seoul, South Korea. Event dates will be November 26-27

We’re planning event with 3 tracks simultaneously. Talk(or Lecture) track, Workshop(or Hands on Lab) track, and BoF/Short lecture track. More details will be available on this website and social media. Save the date, and see you in Seoul!
