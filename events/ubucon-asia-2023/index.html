  • Date: 2023-10-07 ~ 2023-10-08

3rd edition of UbuCon Asia will be held in Surakarta, Indonesia

Announcing UbuCon Asia 2023, this year’s event will be helt at PTIK FKIP UNS in Surakarta, Indonesia. It will be also a joint event with LibreOffice Conf Asia.

Join us from 7 to 8 of October this year, and meet people from Ubuntu & LibreOffice community in Indonesia and other regions of Asia!
